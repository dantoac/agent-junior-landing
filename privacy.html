Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

AgenticJunior is built by AInvirion LLC. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We keep it plain and direct.

What we collect

When you sign up and use AgenticJunior, we collect:

  • Account information — your name and email address from Google OAuth. We never see your Google password.
  • OAuth tokens — access tokens for Gmail and Google Calendar, encrypted at rest using Fernet symmetric encryption. These are required to perform actions on your behalf.
  • Conversation history — messages you send to the assistant via the web dashboard, Telegram, or email. Stored and associated with your account, subject to your retention settings.
  • Tasks, notes, and contacts — data you create inside AgenticJunior. This is your data and it belongs to you.
  • Skill API keys — if you configure third-party skills (e.g. weather, YouTube), your API keys are stored encrypted per-user.
  • Usage metadata — basic logs such as request timestamps and error rates. No tracking pixels, no behavioral advertising profiles.

We do not collect payment information directly. Billing (when it launches) will be handled by a third-party processor.

How we store and protect it

Your data lives in a managed PostgreSQL 16 database on DigitalOcean, with the following protections:

  • Row-Level Security (RLS) — every database query is scoped to your user ID at the database level. No application bug can cause one user's data to leak to another.
  • Encryption at rest — OAuth tokens and API keys are encrypted with Fernet before being written to the database. The encryption key is stored separately from the data.
  • Encryption in transit — all connections use TLS. The API is HTTPS-only.
  • No shared infrastructure — we do not use Redis or any shared in-memory cache that could leak session data between users.

Data retention

Conversation history and agent logs are automatically deleted based on your plan's retention setting:

Plan Retention
Starter 24 hours
Pro 7 days
Ultimate 30 days

Tasks, notes, and contacts are retained until you delete them or close your account. When your account is closed, all data is purged within 30 days. You can request immediate deletion at any time.

Third-party services

AgenticJunior relies on the following third parties to function:

  • OpenAI — your messages are sent to OpenAI's API for LLM processing (GPT-4o-mini / GPT-4o). OpenAI processes these requests but does not use API traffic for model training by default. See OpenAI's privacy policy.
  • Google — used for sign-in (OAuth) and to access Gmail and Google Calendar on your behalf. We request only the scopes you explicitly authorize. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Telegram — if you connect the Telegram bot, messages travel through Telegram's servers. See Telegram's privacy policy.
  • DigitalOcean — our hosting provider. Your data is stored in their managed PostgreSQL service. See DigitalOcean's privacy policy.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers. Ever.

Model training

We do not use your data to train AI models. Your conversations, tasks, notes, and any other content you create in AgenticJunior are never used for training purposes — by us or by any third party we work with.

Your rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access — you can view all your data in the dashboard at any time.
  • Export — you can request a full export of your data by emailing support@ainvirion.com. We'll respond within 5 business days.
  • Deletion — you can delete individual items in the dashboard, or request complete account deletion at any time. We will purge all your data within 30 days of a deletion request.
  • Revoke access — you can revoke AgenticJunior's Google OAuth permissions at any time from your Google Account settings.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies.

  • Authentication token — a JWT stored in localStorage to keep you logged in. Cleared on sign-out.
  • Theme preference — aj-theme stored in localStorage so your dark/light mode choice persists across visits.
  • Language preference — aj-lang stored in localStorage so your EN/ES choice persists.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you by email and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at support@ainvirion.com.
